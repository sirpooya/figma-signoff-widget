Design Sign-Off Widget

This widget helps designers track the sign-off process for their designs. It includes date tracking, role assignments, and status management.

Features:

  • Date Tracking: Automatically fills Finalization Date and Last Revision with current Persian timestamp
  • Role Management: Set names for PM, Design Lead, DSM, and Tech Lead
  • Status Tracking: Monitor approval status with dropdown options

Status Options:

In-Review Approved Rejected
The widget will appear in your Figma canvas. Click the "Sign" button to automatically fill the current dates in Persian format.
